Our Services

From emergency clogs to advanced diagnostics, we provide complete drain cleaning solutions tailored to your home's needs.

Emergency Drain Cleaning

Immediate relief for stubborn clogs in sinks, showers, and toilets. We utilize professional-grade grade machinery to restore flow instantly, 24/7.

Hydro Jetting

Blast away grease, sludge, and tree roots with high-pressure water streams. The most effective way to clean sewer lines to a like-new condition.

Video Camera Inspection

We take the guesswork out of repairs. Our fiber-optic cameras locate exact blockage points, pipe cracks, or collapsed lines without digging.

Sewer Line Repair

From spot repairs to full replacements, our licensed experts handle complex sewer line issues caused by shifting soil or aging infrastructure.

Rooter Service

Mechanical snake solutions designed to cut through tough tree root intrusions and clear obstructions that chemical cleaners can't touch.

Leak Detection

Suspect a slab leak or hidden pipe issue? We use advanced sonic technology to pinpoint invisible leaks before they cause major structural damage.

Don't let a small clog turn into a major headache. Our Plano team is ready to dispatch 24/7.
